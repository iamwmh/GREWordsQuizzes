Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GRE Words Quizzes (the “App”) handles information when you use it. We have designed the App to be private by default: it works without an account, does not show ads, and does not include any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. Your vocabulary library and study progress are stored locally on your device.

Summary: We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. We do not track you across apps or websites. The App stores your data on your device and, when you choose to use voice features, relies on Apple’s on-device and system speech technologies to process your spoken answers.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or any other personally identifying information. Specifically:

  • No personal identifiers are collected by the developer.
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are sent to the developer or any third party.
  • No advertising identifiers are used, and the App does not perform tracking as defined by Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

2. Microphone & Speech Recognition

The core feature of the App is answering vocabulary quizzes by speaking. To make this possible, the App requests permission to use your Microphone and Speech Recognition.

  • The microphone is activated only during a practice session, while the App is actively listening for your spoken answer.
  • Audio is used solely to recognize the word you say and compare it with the correct answer. It is processed in real time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to the developer.
  • The App does not save audio recordings of your voice.
About Apple’s Speech Recognition: The App uses Apple’s Speech framework to convert your speech to text. Depending on your device and language, recognition may run entirely on-device, or a portion of audio may be processed by Apple’s servers to perform recognition, as described by Apple. This processing is handled by Apple under Apple’s Privacy Policy, and the App does not receive or retain that audio. The App prefers on-device recognition where it is supported.

3. Apple Intelligence (On-Device Hint Generation)

On supported devices with Apple Intelligence enabled, the App may use Apple’s on-device Foundation model to generate quiz hints for a word (for example, a rephrased definition or a descriptive clue). This generation happens on your device. The App sends only the target word and its dictionary definition to the on-device model to produce hints; it does not send this data to the developer. If Apple Intelligence is unavailable, the App uses curated, offline hints instead.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally using Apple’s Core Data, on your device only:

  • The vocabulary word bank, including any words you add and the generated hints.
  • Your study statistics (e.g., times a word was seen and answered correctly).
  • Your daily goal setting and your daily study history shown on the calendar.

This data remains on your device. It is not uploaded to the developer. If device backups (such as iCloud Backup) are enabled in your iOS settings, this on-device data may be included in your personal backups, which are governed by Apple and your iCloud settings — not by the developer.

5. How We Use Information

Information is used only to provide the App’s functionality, including:

  • Presenting quizzes and checking your spoken answers.
  • Generating and displaying hints.
  • Tracking your progress toward your daily goal and on the study calendar.

6. Sharing & Selling of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The App contains no third-party advertising networks, marketing SDKs, or analytics providers. The only external processing that may occur is Apple’s system-level speech recognition described in Section 2, performed by Apple as part of the operating system.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, you are in control of it at all times:

  • You can delete individual words from the Word Bank within the App.
  • You can remove all App data by deleting the App from your device, which permanently removes its local database from that device.

8. Permissions Summary

MicrophoneUsed during practice to capture your spoken answer in real time. No audio is stored or sent to the developer.
Speech RecognitionUsed to convert your spoken answer into text to check correctness, via Apple’s Speech framework.
Apple IntelligenceOptional, on supported devices. Generates hints on-device. No data is sent to the developer.
NetworkThe App does not require an account or a developer server. Any network use is limited to Apple’s system speech services where applicable.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience of learners and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App does not collect personal information or include accounts, it does not build profiles of any user, including children.

10. Security

Your data is stored within the App’s sandboxed storage on your device and is protected by iOS’s built-in security and data-protection features. Since no personal data is transmitted to or stored by the developer, there is no developer-side database of your information that could be exposed.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the GDPR or CCPA. Because the developer does not collect or store your personal information, there is no personal data held by the developer to access, correct, export, or delete. You can manage all on-device data directly within the App or by deleting the App.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes will be reflected on this page.
